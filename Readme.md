# ERP Management System

## 📌 Project Overview

This **ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) System** is designed to manage the complete business workflow of an organization including **Purchase, Manufacturing, Sales, Accounts, and Administration**.

The system follows a **role-based workflow** where each department performs its task and the process moves forward with proper approvals. This ensures transparency, operational efficiency, and centralized monitoring of all business activities.

The ERP system helps businesses manage:

* Purchase and vendor management
* Manufacturing operations
* Sales and product distribution
* Financial transactions
* Fund request and approval management
* Vendor and client payment cycles

---

# 🧑‍💼 System Roles

## Super Admin

The **Super Admin** has the highest authority in the system and monitors the entire ERP workflow.

Responsibilities:

* Approve Purchase Orders
* Approve Fund Requests
* Monitor all system activities
* Track department workflows
* Manage financial approvals
* View all operational reports

---

## Admin Departments

### Purchase Admin

Responsibilities:

* Review Purchase Requests (PR)
* Create Purchase Orders (PO)
* Send Purchase Orders to Super Admin for approval

---

### Manufacturing Admin

Responsibilities:

* Verify availability of raw materials
* Approve manufacturing operations
* Monitor production workflow

---

### Sales Admin

Responsibilities:

* Approve product quantities for sales
* Assign product stock to Sales Employees
* Monitor sales activities

---

### Accounts Admin

Responsibilities:

* Manage vendor payments
* Generate invoices for clients
* Send invoice notifications through **WhatsApp**
* Monitor vendor and client payment cycles
* Manage account wallet and financial transactions

---

## Employees

Employees operate under their respective departments and perform operational tasks.

Departments include:

* Purchase Employees
* Manufacturing Employees
* Sales Employees

---

# 🔄 ERP System Workflow

## 1. Purchase Request

The **Purchase Employee** creates a **PR (Purchase Request)** for required raw materials.

The request is submitted to the **Purchase Admin** for review.

---

## 2. Purchase Order Creation

The **Purchase Admin** reviews the purchase request.

If approved, the Purchase Admin creates a **Purchase Order (PO)** and sends it to the **Super Admin**.

---

## 3. Super Admin Approval

The **Super Admin** reviews the Purchase Order and approves it.

---

## 4. Vendor Communication

After approval, the **Accounts Admin** receives the order details and communicates with the **Vendor**.

---

## 5. Raw Material Receipt

The vendor supplies raw materials.

The received materials are recorded in the **ERP system inventory**.

---

## 6. Vendor Payment Cycle

Once the raw materials are received, the **vendor payment cycle begins**.

---

## 7. Manufacturing Approval

The **Manufacturing Admin** verifies the availability of raw materials and approves the manufacturing process.

---

## 8. Product Manufacturing

The **Manufacturing Employees** produce the products and update the ERP system with the **quantity of finished products**.

---

## 9. Transfer to Sales Department

Manufactured products are transferred to the **Sales Admin** for further sales processing.

---

## 10. Sales Approval

The **Sales Admin** approves the quantity of products that can be sold and assigns them to **Sales Employees**.

---

## 11. Product Sales

The **Sales Employees** sell products to customers and record the sales details in the ERP system.

---

## 12. Invoice Generation

The **Accounts Admin** generates the **invoice** and sends it to the client via **WhatsApp notification**.

---

## 13. Client Payment Cycle

After invoice generation, the **client payment cycle begins**.

---

## 14. Payment Collection

The **Accounts Admin** receives the payment from the client and records the transaction in the ERP system.

---

# 💰 Fund Management Workflow

When raw materials need to be purchased, a **fund request process** is initiated.

### Fund Request

1. A **Fund Request** is generated.
2. The request is sent to the **Super Admin**.

### Fund Approval

The **Super Admin** reviews and approves the request.

After approval, funds are added to the **Accounts Admin Wallet**.

### Vendor Payment

The **Accounts Admin** uses the wallet balance to make payments to vendors.
